Improvements to the Applicable Hotel and Restaurant Collective Agreement

Functional scope

This Collective Agreement regulates labor relations between companies engaged in the hotel, restaurant, and catering industry, including fast-food establishments such as BitBurger, and the workers who provide services there.

Workday

The maximum working week will be 40 hours of effective work per week on average, calculated annually. A minimum weekly rest period of one and a half uninterrupted days is established. For part-time workers, the workday will be distributed according to the contract, respecting legal limits.

Vacation

All employees will enjoy a paid annual leave period of 30 calendar business days. The vacation schedule will be set by each company, seeking agreement between the parties and taking into account the needs of the service.

Base Salary and Allowances

The salary tables are attached as Annex I to this agreement. The base salary will be supplemented, where applicable, with bonuses for night shifts, holidays, and seniority, as appropriate and detailed in said annex.

Offenses and sanctions

There are three types of offenses: minor, serious, and very serious. The corresponding sanctions may range from a verbal or written warning to disciplinary dismissal, depending on the severity and repetition of the offense. The disciplinary procedure will guarantee a hearing for the interested party.
